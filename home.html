   PIZZA PARADISE


 
    Pizza Paradise was founded in 1998 by Supun Piyumal,who shared a passion 
    for crafting delicious, high-quality pizzas. Starting from a small,family-owned 
    shop, his dedication to using the freshest ingredients andtraditional recipes quickly
    earned him a loyal customer base. Over the years, Pizza Paradise has grown into a 
    beloved local institution, celebrated for its welcoming atmosphere, innovative menu,
    and unwaveringncommitment to excellence. From our first Margherita to our latest 
    gourmet creations, our journey has been driven by a love for pizza and a desire to bring 
    people together with every slice. At Pizza Paradise, we believe your pizza should be 
    created fresh to order using the highest quality ingredients.
